I’ve been DANCING since I was three years old, and this hobby of mine has been an integral part of my life growing up. I dance various styles, including jazz, tap, modern, ballet, contemporary, and urban. Dance is one of the earliest forms of art that I had experienced, and it plays a big part of why I want to pursue a creative career. I’ve also found my role within the dance community in NYC and have made some of my greatest friends within this community. Dance not only allows me to feel free and express myself, it also allows me to connect with others in the world.
